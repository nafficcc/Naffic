Chapter 1 — Everything Felt Right

Zoye had never expected someone like Brad to notice her. He was loud, confident, and the kind of boy who seemed to belong to the world. And yet, somehow... he picked her. She remembered the first time he called her special. Her heart had thudded in a way that felt dangerous and beautiful all at once.

The relationship bloomed quickly — late-night texts, walking hand-in-hand after class, his jacket over her shoulders when it rained. For once, Zoye stopped questioning if she was enough. Brad made her feel chosen.
